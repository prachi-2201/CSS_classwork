Once upon a time lived a big lion called Mufasa. Mufasa was a big broad lion and he had a brother called Scar. Scar was a bad brother and was jealous of his brother Mufasa. Mufasa and his wife Sarabi had a precious cub and they named him simba. They tort simba to be the bravest and the strongest lion that would had of ever lived, and they also protected him from his uncle scar. There was many other lion and lioness but one nice beautiful day scar and Mufasa were fighting they were very seriously and the wanted each other died. Mufasa sore his son Simba down the galley walking by himself but all the sudden his father see a Hurd of animal running toward simba.
